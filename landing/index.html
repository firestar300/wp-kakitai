Powerful Features

Everything you need to make Japanese text accessible

One-Click Addition

Select Japanese text and add furigana with a single click from the Gutenberg toolbar.

Toggle On/Off

Click again to remove furigana. Perfect for editing and updating content easily.

Smart Detection

Powered by Kuromoji.js for accurate morphological analysis of Japanese text.

Standard HTML

Uses ruby, rt, and rp tags for maximum browser compatibility. No special CSS needed.

Native Integration

Seamlessly integrated into WordPress Gutenberg editor. Works with Paragraph and Heading blocks.

Fast Performance

Client-side processing with intelligent caching. No impact on your site's front-end speed.
