Kanji to Furigana, Instantly

Automatically add furigana (振り仮名) reading guides to Japanese kanji. Perfect for learners, students, and young readers.

See It In Action

From kanji to furigana with one click

Without furigana:

今日は良い天気です。

With furigana:

今日きょうは良よい天気てんきです。

One click • Automatic detection • Standard HTML

Powerful Features

Everything learners need to read Japanese content

One-Click Addition

Select Japanese text and add furigana with a single click from the Gutenberg toolbar.

Toggle On/Off

Click again to remove furigana. Perfect for editing and updating content easily.

Smart Detection

Powered by Kuromoji.js for accurate morphological analysis of Japanese text.

Standard HTML

Uses ruby, rt, and rp tags for maximum browser compatibility. No special CSS needed.

Native Integration

Seamlessly integrated into WordPress Gutenberg editor. Works with Paragraph and Heading blocks.

Fast Performance

Client-side processing with intelligent caching. No impact on your site's front-end speed.

How It Works

Three simple steps to add furigana to your content

1

Select Text

Highlight the Japanese text you want to add furigana to in the Gutenberg editor

2

Click Button

Click the furigana button in the toolbar - it's that simple

3

Done!

Furigana is automatically added to all kanji in your selection
